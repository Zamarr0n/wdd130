Audience

  • Target Customers:
  • Age: 18-45 years old
  • Financial Situation: Middle to upper-middle class
  • Interests: Adventure, outdoor activities, nature exploration, physical fitness, travel enthusiasts.

Question 2: What do they want?

  • Target Customer Desires:
  • Thrilling and memorable experiences.
  • Access to well-organized and safe rafting adventures.
  • High-quality equipment and professional guides.
  • Connection with nature and stunning scenic views.
  • Options for various levels of rafting difficulty.

Question 3: What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

  • Detailed information on rafting trips, including difficulty levels.
  • Transparent pricing, availability, and any special offers.
  • Comprehensive safety measures and qualifications of guides.
  • Visual content like photos and videos showcasing the rafting experience.
  • Contact information and directions to the rafting location.

Question 4: What are their needs that aren't being met?

  • Unmet Customer Needs:
  • Customized rafting experiences catering to different skill levels.
  • Integration of other adventure activities for a holistic adventure package.
  • A sense of community and interaction with like-minded adventure enthusiasts.

Question 5: How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

Your audience, which includes individuals aged 18-55 with diverse interests, will typically access your website through a mix of devices, including desktop computers, laptops, and mobile devices. Therefore, it's crucial to ensure that your website is responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile access.

By addressing the characteristics, desires, expectations, unmet needs, and preferred devices for accessing your website, you can create content and design that effectively appeals to and engages your target audience interested in rafting adventures.
